<Layout title="Creating an item">

Creating an item in your inventory is easy. Just follow these steps:

- Navigate to your inventory by clicking on the "Inventory" button in the side/bottom navigation menu depending on what platform you are on.
- If you are on desktop, click on "Create an item" in the center of the screen. If you are on mobile, click on the "+" button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
- Select the room you want to create an item in.
- **If you want to scan items (easier)**
  - Click on the "Scan" button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  - Scan the items one-by-one using your phone's camera.
  - If you want to scan multiple items at once, you can use tap on the "Infinity" button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
- **If you want to manually enter items (more time consuming)**
  - Click on the "Add" button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  - Enter the item's name and quantity

Great job! You have successfully created an item in your inventory.

<ProTip tip="You can also create an item by clicking on the + button in the bottom right corner of the screen when you are in a room." />
