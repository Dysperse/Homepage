## What is Spaces?
Spaces is a place to store collaborative (or personal!) memos and notes. You can use it to store your thoughts, ideas, and more. Spaces can be useful for a variety of things, such as:
- Storing links to articles you want to read
- Storing images you might want to use later
- Sharing important images with your group, such as shopping receipts or photos of your pet

## How to create a memo

You can create a memo by clicking on the "Spaces" tab in the sidebar. You can quickly press CTRL+V and Dysperse will automatically create a memo for you.

## How do I attach a file to a memo?

Click on the image icon in the editor. You can then attach a file from your device
